Services

What I can ship for you

Whether you’re buried in invoices, fighting double data entry, or trying to connect a new ERP, we’ll scope a concrete integration that pays for itself in saved time and fewer errors.

01 · AP automation pipeline
From messy inbox to clean, structured data

A done-for-you workflow that turns receipts and invoices from email or scans into structured data ready to post into your accounting or ERP system.

  • Email inbox or upload folder for invoices and receipts
  • Parsing & validation for vendor, date, totals, tax, and key fields
  • Exports or API pushes into QuickBooks, Quickbase, or your ERP
  • Basic duplicate detection and simple approval routing
Typical scope: initial build + light monthly support. Ideal for teams processing recurring vendor bills or job expenses.
02 · ERP & Quickbase integrations
Keep systems in sync without manual exports

Custom integrations for Acumatica, Quickbase, ERPNext and related tools, designed around how your data and teams already work.

  • Sync customers, vendors, jobs, or projects between systems
  • Push approved invoices from Quickbase into your ERP
  • Bring meter/usage or field data into a central system of record
  • Handle awkward edge cases with custom business rules
Good fit if you’re tired of CSV imports/exports and want one reliable source of truth.
03 · Custom automation & middleware
Glue code for the real-world stack you already use

Not every problem needs a new SaaS subscription. Sometimes you just need solid, boring automation that’s built once and maintained well.

  • Google Workspace & email automation
  • File watchers, ETL jobs, and scheduled scripts
  • Data quality checks before it hits finance or ops
  • Simple dashboards or reports on top of existing data
If you’re saying “there has to be a better way than this spreadsheet,” this is for you.

Process

How we go from “idea” to “live and boring”

The goal is not flashy prototypes. The goal is stable, low-drama automations that keep running quietly in the background and are easy to understand.

01
Discovery & quick audit
We walk through how you currently handle invoices, receipts, and data. I map where information starts, where it needs to end up, and where mistakes tend to happen.
02
Concrete proposal
You get a short, plain-English plan: systems involved, what will be automated, what stays manual, and a fixed or not-to-exceed price.
03
Build & iterate with real data
I wire things up in a safe test environment, using real but controlled data so we can see how the automation behaves before it touches production.
04
Launch & steady-state support
We go live, add guardrails, and set up light monitoring. You get documentation, access to the code, and an option for ongoing support as needed.
